My OC's name is Lady. Due to her nature, no one knows what she is, a human or a cursed spirit. She feeds off of her target's love for her. Lady has the ability to shapeshift and switch her appearance to match said target's type. Usually she doesn't stay too long with someone, but Naoya is a very unique case. So she made and exception for him. That exception, however, will leave her to stay with him 'til death does them part.
